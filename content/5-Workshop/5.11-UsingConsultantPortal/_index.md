## Using the Consultant Portal

The Consultant Portal is a dedicated interface for consultants to manage their appointments and view their schedules.

### Accessing the Consultant Portal

1. Open the Consultant Portal URL from the `outputs.json` file
2. Log in with your consultant email and password
3. First-time users will need to change their temporary password

{{% notice info %}}
Consultant accounts are created by administrators through the Admin Dashboard. You will receive your login credentials via email.
{{% /notice %}}

### My Appointments Page

The **My Appointments** page is your central hub for managing all your scheduled meetings.

#### View Appointments

See all your appointments in a table view with:
- Customer name and contact
- Appointment date and time
- Status (Pending, Confirmed, Completed, Cancelled)
- Notes and special requests

#### Filter Appointments

Use the filter options to narrow down your view:
- **By Status**: Show only pending, confirmed, or completed appointments
- **By Date Range**: View appointments for specific time periods
- **Search**: Find appointments by customer name

#### Appointment Actions

For each appointment, you can:

**View Details:**
- Click on an appointment to see full customer information
- Review appointment notes
- Check booking history

**Confirm Appointments:**
- Change status from Pending to Confirmed
- Customer receives automatic email notification

**Complete Appointments:**
- Mark appointments as Completed after the meeting
- Add session notes (optional)

**Cancel Appointments:**
- Cancel appointments with a reason
- System sends cancellation email to customer

{{% notice warning %}}
You cannot directly modify appointment times or dates. Contact your administrator if changes are needed.
{{% /notice %}}

#### Email Notifications

You'll receive automatic emails for:
- New appointment bookings
- Appointment cancellations
- Appointment updates
- Reminders 24 hours before appointments

### My Schedule Page

The **My Schedule** page displays your weekly availability.

#### Weekly View

- See your time slots organized by day of week
- Color-coded slot status:
  - **Green**: Available
  - **Yellow**: Booked
  - **Red**: Blocked/Unavailable

#### Slot Information

Each time slot shows:
- Day of week
- Start and end time
- Current status
- Upcoming appointment (if booked)

#### View-Only Schedule

{{% notice info %}}
Consultants have **read-only** access to their schedules. All schedule modifications must be made by administrators through the Admin Dashboard.
{{% /notice %}}

If you need schedule changes:
1. Note the specific slots you want to modify
2. Contact your administrator via email or the dashboard messaging
3. Provide clear details about desired changes

#### Schedule Conflicts

If you see a conflict or error in your schedule:
- Take a screenshot
- Note the date and time
- Report to administrator immediately
- Do not confirm appointments during conflict periods

### Dashboard Features

#### Profile Settings

Access your profile from the top-right menu:
- View your personal information
- Update contact details
- Change password
- Upload profile picture

{{% notice tip %}}
Keep your profile updated with current contact information so customers can reach you if needed.
{{% /notice %}}

#### Statistics Overview

See your performance metrics:
- Total appointments this month
- Completed appointments
- Cancellation rate
- Customer ratings (if enabled)

#### Support & Help

Access help resources:
- User guide
- Contact administrator
- Report technical issues
- FAQ section

### Best Practices

**Managing Appointments:**
- Review new appointments daily
- Confirm appointments promptly
- Add notes after each session for continuity
- Update statuses in real-time

**Communication:**
- Respond to customer messages quickly
- Notify admin of any scheduling issues immediately
- Keep contact information current
- Use professional language in notes

**Schedule Management:**
- Check your schedule at the start of each week
- Plan for buffer time between appointments
- Request schedule updates at least 48 hours in advance
- Block out personal time through your administrator

### Troubleshooting

**Issue: Cannot log in**
- Verify you're using the correct Consultant Portal URL (not Admin Dashboard)
- Check if your account is active with administrator
- Reset password if needed

**Issue: Appointments not showing**
- Refresh the page
- Check your date range filter settings
- Verify you're looking at the correct status filter

**Issue: Email notifications not received**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address in your profile
- Contact administrator to check SES configuration

{{% notice tip %}}
The Consultant Portal is optimized for mobile devices. You can access it on your phone to check appointments on the go!
{{% /notice %}}

You're now ready to effectively manage your consulting appointments through the Consultant Portal!
