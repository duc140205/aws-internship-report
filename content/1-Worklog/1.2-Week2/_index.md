### Week 2 Objectives:

* Understand and practice deploying **Amazon EC2**: AMI, EBS, Instance Store, Metadata, Auto Scaling, Pricing.  
* Learn related Compute & Storage services: Lightsail, EFS, FSx, MGN and real-world use cases.  
* Practice launching EC2 instances and managing resources with **Tags & Resource Groups**.  
* Get familiar with and use **Amazon CloudWatch** for monitoring & logging.  
* Practice deploying applications with **Auto Scaling Group (ASG)** to ensure availability and scalability.  
* Learn the **AWS Transit Gateway** mechanism for connecting multiple VPCs.
